Dear Reader,

My name is Angela Ka-Wai Mak and I am currently a university student at the University of Waterloo. I am currently in my first term of 4th year as a Psychology Major in the Arts and Business program. I am also specializing in Human Resource Management.

I am the kind of person who is full of energy, optimistic, and always ready for an adventure. I like to take challenges head on and I will always persevere to reach my goals.

I enjoy travelling and exploring places that I have never been. I have spent a lot of time in Asia in the past few years and I hope to head to Europe on my next excursion!

I think that I had a very difficult beginning to my professional career. In my first work term that was scheduled for Winter 2013, I did not get employed even after having many interviews. I was very nervous, shy, and scared. It was such a big shock for me that it pushed me to step outside of my shell.

Coming from such a tough position, I now have 4 work terms and 3 companies under my belt. I am very proud of where I am today and how hard I have worked for it.
