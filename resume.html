Private Piano Teaching



In my final year of high school, I was requested by a family friend to teach her daughter piano. For the period of the summer inbetween Grade 12 and First Year University, I taught her once a week.




We have had a very long relationship because I have known her since she was born. We bonded over our love for piano and I am glad that I helped to keep her interest in it.
