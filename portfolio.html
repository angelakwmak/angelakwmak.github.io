ARBUS 200 Project



This project spanned the entire length of the term and is one of my most intensive projects. I had two main jobs in this project. I was in charge of designing the product and coming up with the design of the product. I was also in charge of all the visuals of the project as well as formatting of the document (because I was most familiar with microsoft word and familiar with photo-editing).

I remember that the start of our project was rough. We were in discussion for over 2 weeks and we could not agree on the product we were proposing.

To remedy this, we hosted one intensive brainstorming session that lasted about 5 to 6 hours. We first listed out every possible idea we could come up with on a white board. We then looked at each and every option to discuss the possibilities of each. After the discussion process, we each had our top 3 ideas in our minds. We held a vote to decide the product we were going with.

In order for our proposal to be cohesive, we read through all 56 pages together to make edits and change any sentencing when necessary. It was a long process, but we stuck it through in order to prepare (what we believed to be) a top quality report.
